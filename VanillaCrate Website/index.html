About VanillaCrate

VanillaCrate is a lightweight Minecraft Survival server created with one goal: Bring back the feeling of playing Minecraft with friends. No overwhelming menus. No unnecessary features. No complicated systems. Just survival, building, exploration, community events and making unforgettable memories. Every feature has been chosen carefully to keep the gameplay clean, enjoyable and as close to vanilla Minecraft as possible while still improving the overall experience.

Server Features

Pure Survival

Enjoy a mostly vanilla experience where progression feels rewarding and every build tells a story.

Community Town

Visit a shared town where players can meet, trade, relax and build together with very few restrictions.

Arena Events

Take part in exciting PvP events, challenges and seasonal competitions for fun rewards.

Performance First

VanillaCrate focuses on smooth gameplay using carefully selected lightweight plugins and server optimizations.

Friendly Community

Whether you're a builder, explorer or casual player, everyone has a place here.

Future Updates

New community events, improvements and quality-of-life additions will continue after launch.
